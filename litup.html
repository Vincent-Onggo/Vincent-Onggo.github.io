LitUp Network Internship

Overview

As a Software Engineering Intern at LitUp Network, a subsidiary of OMS Group (a global, neutral, and integrated telecommunications infrastructure service provider), in Jakarta, I had the opportunity to work on real-world challenges in data visualization and remote infrastructure management. I developed a Tkinter-based application to streamline building management system (BMS) data analysis and played a key role in setting up a new cable landing station in Batam. Additionally, I contributed to cost-saving measures by setting up self-hosted RustDesk servers for secure, remote access to infrastructure across multiple sites.

Role: Software Engineering Intern

Duration: May 2024 — July 2024

The Challenge

Managing and visualizing large-scale BMS data efficiently was a major hurdle. With hundreds of parameters tracked over months, engineers needed a more intuitive way to interpret trends and insights. Additionally, ensuring secure remote access to BMS across different locations was crucial for seamless maintenance and monitoring.

My Approach

To tackle these challenges, I developed a Tkinter-based tool that utilized Excel data input and Matplotlib for dynamic visualizations. This provided engineers with a clear and structured view of complex BMS data. Additionally, I set up self-hosted RustDesk servers to enable secure, remote desktop access to monitor and manage the infrastructure, significantly reducing the company's reliance on paid subscription services like TeamViewer.

Impact

The project significantly improved LitUp’s operational efficiency. Engineers gained a more intuitive way to analyze BMS data, leading to faster decision-making. The self-hosted RustDesk solution provided secure remote access, allowing teams to troubleshoot and manage systems from anywhere without incurring subscription costs. My contributions also played a key role in the successful setup of the Batam cable landing station, furthering LitUp's mission to build reliable digital infrastructure in Indonesia.

Key Takeaways

This experience reinforced the importance of adaptability, problem-solving, and effective communication. I honed my technical skills in Python, Tkinter, and RustDesk configuration while also learning how to navigate real-world project constraints. Collaborating with professionals across different teams gave me valuable insights into network management, data visualization, and the impact of well-designed tools on daily operations.
