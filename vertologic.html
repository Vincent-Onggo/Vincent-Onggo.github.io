Vertilogic - Virtue-X Client

Overview

At Vertilogic, a software solutions company, I partnered with a development team to optimize key backend features for Virtue-X, a virtual 360° exhibition platform. The platform allowed exhibitors and visitors to engage through a fully immersive online experience. Virtue-X became an essential tool during the COVID-19 pandemic, providing businesses a way to connect with their audiences remotely when in-person events were no longer feasible.

Role: Intern Web App Developer, Product Development

Duration: June 2021 — July 2021

View demo

The Challenge

User Problem

Exhibitors and visitors were struggling to connect effectively during the pandemic, with traditional exhibition formats restricted or canceled. Businesses were unable to showcase their offerings, while customers couldn’t interact with new products and services. The need for an engaging and accessible digital platform became clear, one that could simulate the exhibition experience while maintaining interaction and engagement.

Our Approach

Solution

As part of the development team, I worked on optimizing the backend of Virtue-X, focusing on real-time data synchronization using WebSockets. I also helped improve database management with MongoDB, which efficiently handled over 10,000 exhibitor and visitor records. Key features of the platform included virtual booths, live chat, and video conferencing, allowing exhibitors to present their products dynamically. The platform also provided valuable data analytics, helping businesses tailor their engagement strategies based on user activity.

Impact

Impact

Virtue-X enabled businesses to maintain visibility during a time when physical interactions were not possible. The platform facilitated meaningful connections between exhibitors and visitors, extending the reach of events beyond geographical limitations. Feedback from users showed high satisfaction, and the platform played a key role in the digital transformation of events, allowing businesses to continue operations and engage with a wider audience.

Key Takeaways

Takeaways

This project provided hands-on experience in real-time communication features and enhanced my technical skills with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. It also emphasized the importance of rapid problem-solving and adaptability in a fast-paced development environment. I learned how critical it is to incorporate user feedback and take a user-centered approach in product development, especially when creating a platform meant to serve a wide range of people with varying needs.
